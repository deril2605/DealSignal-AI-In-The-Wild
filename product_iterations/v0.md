# DealSignal MVP v0

## Scope

Watchlist plus daily pipeline execution:

- ingest a watchlist of companies with execs and themes
- discover public sources
- fetch new URLs and archive raw text
- extract structured strategic signals
- deduplicate events
- score and store events in SQLite
- expose results in the local UI

`reports/daily_digest.md` is generated as an internal artifact, but digest delivery is not part of `v0`.

## What Exists

- `serve/` contains the full local runtime
- `azure_artifacts/` contains ACA deployment assets
- ACA job runs on schedule and persists SQLite state to Blob
- raw fetched article text is uploaded to Blob
- local `serve` pulls the latest DB snapshot from Blob on startup
- admin page exists for pipeline and ACA visibility

## Current Persistence

- DB blob: `state/dealsignal.db`
- raw article blobs: `raw/<sha>.txt`

## Current Watchlist Status

The watchlist has been expanded beyond the original single-company setup and is ready for broader validation, but `v0` should still be treated as a functional MVP rather than a scale-proven system for 50 to 200 entities.

## Success Criteria Met

- end-to-end scheduled run works in ACA
- structured events are stored and visible in UI
- dedupe and scoring are functioning
- state survives across ACA executions

## Not Included In v0

- digest delivery to email, Slack, or Teams
- advanced change-detection or alerting logic
- scale hardening for large watchlists
- Azure-native monitoring beyond current operational visibility

## Next Candidate Iteration

`v1`: change detection and alerting based on material deltas in company narrative.
